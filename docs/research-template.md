# Game Research Template

Use this template when applying the AI game-research workflow to a new topic.

## Research Topic

Describe the game, genre, mechanic, player-experience question, or design problem being researched.

## Research Question

What decision should this research help answer?

Example:

> How should a small team design a beginner-friendly fighting-game tutorial without watering down advanced mechanics?

## Context

Add relevant background:

- target audience;
- game genre;
- platform;
- comparable games;

- design goals; and
- assumptions.

## Human Review Checklist

Before treating the research as final, check:

- [ ] Are the recommendations specific enough to act on?
- [ ] Are claims connected to sources or examples?
- [ ] Are tradeoffs and risks acknowledged?
- [ ] Would a designer, programmer, or producer understand the conclusion?
- [ ] Is anything overclaimed beyond the evidence?
